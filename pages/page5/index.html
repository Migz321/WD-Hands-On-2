Naruto and sasuke


Sasuke channels the sealed tailed beasts' chakra into his Susanoo. Kurama marvels at how similar and different Sasuke is from Hagoromo, and warns Naruto not to let his guard down. Sasuke's enhanced Susanoo takes shape, and Naruto creates shadow clones, all of them in Tailed Beast Mode. Sasuke derides Naruto for using it, seeing it as a cover for Naruto's loneliness. Sasuke sends a couple of the clones crashing down, while cutting down limbs from others.
